User Guide for Strong Password Generator

Creating a password with default settings

- The default length of the password generator is 12 characters long and includes numbers, special symbols, and uppercase letters.

- Click on the "Generate Password" button to create a default password.

- Once generated, clik on the "Copy" button to copy the password. This will store the password in your device's clipboard, allowing you to paste it later.

Creating a custom password

- To create a custom password of length other than 12, you can increase or decrease the value in the range of 4 to 20.

- If you wish to exclude any of the options such as uppercase, symbols or numbers - simply uncheck them before generating your password.

- Click on the "Generate Password" button to create a the password.

- Once generated, clik on the "Copy" button to copy the password. This will store the password in your device's clipboard, allowing you to paste it later.

Recommendations and Tips

- It's recommended to keep all the options (uppercase, special symbols, and numbers) checked and to set the password length to a minimum of 12 characters.

- It is also a good habit to use a password manager to manage and store passwords.

- You should be changing your passwords every 3-4 months and should not be using the same password for more than one account.
